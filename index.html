Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the FlashAi mobile application (Android).

Welcome to FlashAi. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Use Google Sign-In authentication
  • Contact us for support
  • Use our services

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (from Google account)
  • User ID and authentication tokens

1.2 Images and Media

Our app allows you to:

  • Upload images from your device gallery
  • Capture photos using your device camera
  • Process and enhance jewelry images using AI
  • Save processed images to your device

Images you upload are processed for AI-powered jewelry photoshoot enhancements. We store your images securely in Firebase Storage associated with your account.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data and analytics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Network connectivity information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered jewelry photoshoot services
  • Authentication: To create and manage your account using Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In
  • Image Processing: To process your jewelry images using our AI technology
  • Storage: To store your images and collections in Firebase Storage and Firestore
  • Communication: To send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and improve user experience
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data security

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Firebase Services: We use Google Firebase for secure authentication, storage, and database services
  • Supabase: Additional data is stored securely in Supabase with encryption
  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Control: Strict access controls limit who can access your data

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication purposes
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, cloud storage, and database services
  • Supabase: For additional backend services
  • Razorpay: For payment processing (if applicable)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture jewelry photos for AI processing
  • Storage/Photos: To access and save images from your device gallery
  • Internet: To communicate with our servers and process images
  • Network State: To check connectivity status

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and images for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at support@flashai.co.in from your registered email address.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

8. Children's Privacy

“FlashAi is not designed for use by children and does not knowingly collect data from children.”
